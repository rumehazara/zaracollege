About Us

Zara College of Distance Education is a government-recognized educational institution based in Hyderabad, Telangana. We are committed to providing quality education through flexible learning opportunities for students who cannot attend regular schools and colleges.

Our college offers Open 10th, Intermediate, and Undergraduate Degree programs in English, Telugu, and Urdu mediums. Whether you’re a school dropout, a working professional, or someone looking to complete your education later in life — we are here to support your goals.

With affordable fees, experienced faculty, and personal guidance in every step, Zara College aims to make education accessible for everyone regardless of background, age, or language.

We believe in second chances and the power of knowledge to change lives.
